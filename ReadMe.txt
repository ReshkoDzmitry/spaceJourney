Space Jorney - is a game in which you have to overcome the asteroid belt.
Your path will be difficult and dangerous. Ahead of you is the unknown space, the bewitching beauty of galaxies and other worlds.

Control:
mouse movement + left mouse button for single shot.

Interface:
SCORE- progress of other players.
POINTS - the number of points scored.
LEVEL - the level of difficulty of the game.
LIFE - the number of lives. Maximum 5.

Collect hearts, because they not only restore lives, but also give +5 points.
Bonus - gives +10 points.

Link: https://reshkodzmitry.github.io/spaceJourney/


The game is written using Canvas technology.
